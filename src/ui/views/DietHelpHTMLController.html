Diet Management: Help

General Description

This window is used to visualize all the data from one diet.

Operations

Open windows

To open the windows, click on the corresponding button in the application's side menu.

Logout

To logout, click on the User button next to to the icon        at the bottom left of the window, later a menu will be displayed and click Log out button. Confirmation will be requested before log out.

Modify User

To Modify User, click on the User button next to to the icon        at the bottom left of the window, later a menu will be displayed and click Profile button.

Exit App

To exit the app, click on the X icon in the upper right corner of the window. Confirmation will be requested before exitting the app.

Help

Click on the icon        on the upper left side of the window.
